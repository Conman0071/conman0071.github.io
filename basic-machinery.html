Metallurgic Infuser:

To start off in mekanism there are a few vital machines that will be used throughout your mekanism experience.
Before crafting these machines though, youll need to have a good amount of osmium, redstone and iron. Once
you have these resources, the first machine to craft is the metallurgic infuser. This allows you to make
basic circuits as well as all of the alloys.

Power Generation:

You will need some way of powering this machine, a simple coal generator from any mod will do the trick, if you
dont have any other mods than the heat generator is the only generator you can craft. These Generators are far
from efficient and will only suffice for the very basics of the mod. Once you have progressed further into the
mod you will create other means of power generation. See The ore production page for more info on better power.

Circuits and Alloys:

Once powered up, put some redstone in the left most slot and iron into the left middle slot and congratulations
you have made your first enriched alloy. Replace the iron with osmium and you have made a basic circuit. These
two items are the backbone of the mod and will be used for nearly every recipe.
