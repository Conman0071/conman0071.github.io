Ore Production

Overview Basic Machinery Ore Production

Introduction:

Mekanism allows you to refine ore to get more than one ingot per ore. There are 4 stages to the production, each
stage allows you to gain one extra ingot from the ore. Each stage uses the machines from the previous stage and
adds on another or multiple machines. This is extremely useful for the Mekanism mod as well as other mods that
require an abundance of metals. Some ores from other mods are not supported in the later stages of production due
to some of the machines not accepting modded ores, however all of the base game ores are able to be processed
and stage 2 works for most modded ores. All of the machines in this process will require a good power source.

Stage 1:

Stage 1 is the most basic stage and will only provide you with one ingot per ore. The only benefit you will gain
from using this method over a vanilla furnace is that the production is faster. This stage simply uses an energised
smelter to smelt ore into one ingot.

Stage 2:

Stage 2 is a little more complicated but still very simple, This stage will give you two ingots from one ore. This
is similar to many other mods that implement ore processing. For this stage you will need the energised smelter
again as well as an enrichment chamber which will turn one ore into two dust as long as the ore you are refining
is supported by the mod. All of the ores that have a dustr form from other mods should also be compatible in this
stage.

Stage 3:

Stage 3 is where it really starts to implement other aspects of the mod, you will need the other two machines from
stage two as well as three new machines, the electrolytic seperator, the purification chamber and the crusher. lots
of mods have a crusher so make sure you are crafting the Mekanism one, if you are using a modpack. First, you
need to pump water into the electrolytic seperator, this machine seperates the water into oxygen and hydrogen.
For this stage we need the oxygen. Pump the oxygen out of the seperator into the purification chamber, this will
turn the ore into 3 clumps, then the crusher will crush the three clumps into 3 dirty dust. The enrichment chamber
will turn the dirty dust into regular dust which can be smelted in the energised smelter. This stage is where most
ores will not be able to be processed as the clumps and dirty dust are a mekanism specific item.

Salination Tower:

Stage 4 will require a whole new structure, you will need a salination tower to make brine. The salination
tower works on heat. You will need to build it in a hot biome such as a volcanic island or a desert biome.
The more heat you have the faster it will produce brine. To build the tower you will create a four by four
block of thermal evaporation blocks. next you will build the middle blocks, they can be from 1 to 16 high
and must include at least 2 thermal evaporation valves on any level. The 2x2 in the center must be hollow.
The top layer consists of four advanced solar panels for power, one in each corner and a thermal evaporation
controller somewhere in the layer and the rest made up of thermal evaporation blocks. Input water into one
of the valves, this will use a large amount of water so make sure you have a good source. Brine will now be
produced, export the brine through the other valve and you have completed this stage.

Stage 4:

You will need some other machines to refine the ore as well as the other machines from the previous stages. You
will need another two electrolytic seperators one to put the brine into and one for water. You will also need a
chemical infuser. To start this stage, pump the chlorine made in the seperator from the brine, into one side of
the chemical infuser and pump the hydrogen made in the other seperator from the water into the other side.
This will mix the two chemicals and produce hydrogen chloride. Pump the hydrogen chloride from the chemical
infuser into a chemical injection chamber. This is the start point for your ore, put your ore into the injection
chamber to produce ore shards. The shards then go into the purification chamber from stage 3 and are made
into four clumps then the clumpsgo into the crusher to make dirty dust then into the enrichment chamber which
will make dust then the dustis smelted. This is a very complicated stage and requires good knowledge of the mod
and how it works. It isalso a very power hungry stage and will require a good power source especially with upgrades.
